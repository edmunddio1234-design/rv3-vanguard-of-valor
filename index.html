Who Your Gift Reaches

Rural veterans, their dependents, and their survivors

More than 4 million U.S. veterans live in rural areas — roughly one in four. They tend to be older, more likely to live with disabilities, and farther from every service they earned.

The veteran in an isolated parish

Limited transportation, scarce medical facilities, and thin social networks mean many rural veterans simply miss out on the benefits, healthcare, and employment they earned. Your gift funds the mobile outreach and navigation that closes that distance.

The spouse and dependents standing beside them

Eligible family members — spouses and dependents under 21 — are served alongside the veteran through the ecosystem's navigation and support pathways, from claims readiness to housing stability to family legal-readiness documents.

The survivor navigating what comes after

Survivor benefits are among the most under-claimed in the federal system. RV3 support helps fund survivor intake, documentation, and benefit-uptake pathways — and the Guardian Light memorial programs that ensure no family stands alone.

The veteran ready to build something

39% of veteran entrepreneurs live in rural areas, and veteran-led firms show higher five-year survival than average. Bootcamp scholarships funded through RV3 turn service-built discipline into community-rooted enterprises.

How Funds Flow

From your gift to a veteran's doorstep

RV3 channels contributions into the ecosystem's proven delivery programs — each with a named operator, a defined outcome, and VI-PAR reporting.

Outreach

M-TOC deployments

The Mobile Triage Outreach Cavalry brings triage, benefits navigation, and crisis stabilization directly to remote parishes — the ecosystem's "front door" on wheels.

Housing

Transitional housing & homeownership

Support for LCDC's housing pathway and Operation Eagle's Nest — moving veterans from housing insecurity through transitional units into owned homes.

Opportunity

Bootcamp scholarships

Full and partial scholarships to the six-week Eagle Vanguard Venture Boot Camp, prioritized for veterans facing rural, disability, and access barriers.

Legacy

Memorial & honor programs

The Guardian Light Veterans Legacy Memorial, named tributes, dedication ceremonies, and the Legacy Activation Fund's scholarships and service projects.

Featured Programs

Three flagships your support makes possible

Housing to Homeownership

Operation Eagle's Nest

A phased pipeline that turns transitional homes into "eagle's nests" — pairing rent-stable housing with mandatory HUD-approved homebuyer education, financial counseling, and purchase incentives, so veterans leave the program as homeowners, not returnees to instability.

How the pipeline works
Navigation & Readiness

Eagle Vanguard VAPE Network

A tiered veteran-and-family support model that cuts through fragmentation, delays, and paperwork burden — combining navigation, vetted referrals, legal readiness, and premium action planning for high-need cases. RV3 underwrites access for those who cannot pay.

Inside the network
Mobile Outreach

M-TOC — Mobile Triage Outreach Cavalry

When a veteran cannot reach services, the services ride out to the veteran. Sponsored deployment days put counselors, screeners, and benefit navigators on the ground in the Florida Parishes, Acadiana, and beyond.
