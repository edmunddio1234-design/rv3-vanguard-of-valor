A Note for Veterans & Families

If you need help, not a giving page

RV3 is the ecosystem's funding arm — but no veteran who reaches us gets a "wrong department" answer. If you or a family member needs benefits navigation, housing support, or crisis stabilization, contact us anyway. We will personally hand you to the right team at Lighthouse CDC or the Eagle Enterprises Consortium and make sure the hand-off actually happens.

For direct services, you can also start at the Lighthouse Rural Communities main site, which carries the full program directory, or call the shared line at (504) 505-1512.

Response Standards

What to expect after you write

  • Acknowledgment of your message within two business days.
  • Veteran-assistance messages are routed same-day whenever received during business hours.
  • Sponsorship and funder inquiries go straight to the partnerships lead.
  • Tribute and memorial inquiries are handled by one person, start to finish, with the care they deserve.
