How We Build Packages

Discovery first. Grids second.

Our sponsorship program follows the discipline of the professional sponsorship playbook our team trains on: define the property as a marketing opportunity (not just a need), know the audience in real data points, and build activations around what sponsors' audiences actually want — not logos on signs.

That means the first meeting is about your goals — the communities you want to reach, the causes your employees rally behind, the outcomes your brand wants to stand beside. We ask questions for most of the meeting, then build the sponsorship proposal together. The tier levels below are honest starting frameworks with real benefits — and every final package is customized in discovery.

What you are sponsoring is substantive: a working ecosystem serving rural veterans across four Louisiana regions, with pilot activity of 312 veterans reached and 184 completed referrals (pilot demonstration data), a public VI-PAR outcomes dashboard, and communications reach through Guardian Angels' website, app, podcast, newsletter, and social channels.

What Every Sponsor Receives

Baseline commitments at all levels

  • A written activation plan with named deliverables and dates.
  • Outcome reporting tied to the VI-PAR framework — you see what your sponsorship moved.
  • Recognition governed by our dignity guardrail: visibility that honors veterans, never exploits them.
  • A single relationship manager across the whole ecosystem — one call, four entities.
  • Renewal review built on results, so year two is a decision made on evidence.

Premier Flagship Sponsorship

Name the M-TOC unit. Ride with the cavalry.

The Mobile Triage Outreach Cavalry is the most visible object in the ecosystem — a crewed outreach unit that rolls into rural parishes carrying benefit navigators, health screeners, and counselors. The M-TOC Unit Naming Sponsorship places your name on that mission for a full program year.

  • Unit naming & livery presence — "The [Your Name] Mobile Triage Outreach Cavalry" across unit signage, deployment materials, and route announcements.
  • Every deployment is your event — parish-by-parish visibility at partner sites across the Florida Parishes, Greater Baton Rouge, Greater New Orleans, and Acadiana.
  • Full media integration — featured across Guardian Angels' podcast, newsletter, and social channels, plus dashboard-verified deployment outcomes with your name on the report.
  • Employee activation — reserved volunteer slots for your team on deployment days.

The naming sponsorship is priced through market valuation in discovery — because that is how serious sponsorships are built.

Why naming the M-TOC matters

Mobile outreach is the founding plan's first objective — the "front door" for rural communities. When the unit arrives, it is often the first coordinated veteran service a parish has seen in years. The name on that unit is the name a community remembers as the one that showed up.

One sponsor. One unit. One year of showing up, documented deployment by deployment.
